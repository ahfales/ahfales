research

Here are some of my research projects:

  • "Unpacking a Digital Library": reflecting on my digital research internship with the American Antiquarian Society, at Past is Present
  • "Melville's Scrivener": an article on bibliography, literary history, and Elizabeth Melville, at the Journal of the History of Ideas's blog
  • Both of these articles are related to my undergraduate thesis, "Herman Melville's Body: Archives, Absence, and Historical Literary Practice," which explores literary critical and historical methodology through the life and work of Herman Melville. I seek to recover figures lost in the narrative that twentieth-century literary historians recreated in the wake of Herman Melville's death and scholarly recovery.
